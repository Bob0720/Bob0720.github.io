About Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computation, information, and automation. It spans theoretical disciplines such as algorithms, theory of computation, and information theory to applied disciplines, including the design and implementation of hardware and software. Key areas include cryptography, computer security, computer graphics, computational geometry, programming language theory, database theory, human-computer interaction, software engineering, operating systems, networks, embedded systems, computer architecture, artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.
